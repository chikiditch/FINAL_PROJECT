RAW    DAWGS

Meet RAWDAWGS—the real deal from UE Manila, holding it down under Sigbin Productions. This underground MC spits bars that cut deep, riding sick beats that shake the pavement. He's not here for the radio play; RAWDAWGS is all about raw, hard-hitting truth and that homie-loving lyricism that speaks to the streets. The verses are a blend of grit and soul, a testament to life’s struggles and the fire of ambition. Step up and feel the impact—a revolution in every rhyme.
